I am a formal semanticist interested in the interaction between semantics and its syntactic and pragmatic interfaces. Currently I am a Beatriu de Pinós Research Fellow in the Center for Theoretical Linguistics, at the Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona. I graduated from UMass in 2017, after which I spent two years as a post-doctoral researcher at Henrich-Heine University in Düsseldorf, followed by a Mellon Postdoctoral Fellowship in the Department of Linguistics at Cornell University.

Broadly speaking, my research focuses on (i) how semantic composition is constrained by the syntactic structure of sentences, and (ii) how the output of the semantic computation is affected by the context of utterance. In the past I have mainly explored these questions by looking at quantification and plurality related phenomena. Topics that I have worked on include numerals, comparatives, superlatives and other degree expressions, genericity and kind predication, focus association and ignorance implicatures.
